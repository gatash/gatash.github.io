ABOUT ME


My name is Alexander Gatash. I’m 33 years old. I’m the Architect, Interior & Product Designer, 3D Artist. I was born and I do still live in Ukraine. At the moment, for two years now I live in Lviv.


I have been interested in painting and architecture since the early age. My childhood was spent in Odessa historic center, around the architecture monuments, so thanks to that I decided to be an architect in the early age. When I graduated the school, I entered the preparatory department for the specialty of architect in Odessa State Academy of Civil Engineering and Architecture in 2004 year. After graduating from the first course in 2006, I got my first job at the architect-draftsman, and I have always worked in various architectural studios, combining work with study in subsequent years studying at the university. Also, I studied computer graphics with a great effort as a tool to express my creative ideas. In the fourth year in 2009 year I temporarily suspended studies at the university, because I felt that it would be useful in this period to acquire practical skills in a real work. In 2012 I decided to move to Kiev for much more interesting and challenging projects after working as the architect and interior designer for three years in Odessa. In Kiev, I worked on a full-time job for three years as a concept architect and designer in several studios. The next stage of my professional advancement was the independent provision of design and computer graphics services as a freelancer.


Nowadays I’ve worked over the large number of objects of different scales from interior items to large architectural complexes. Well-formed and organized system of computer graphics tools and a refined structure of the workflow allows me to create in the field of aesthetics and the concept of architecture and design effectively and freely. Creating of something beautiful, and necessarily qualitative, fascinates me the most.
